Course Description

At Vidhyashala Academy, we understand the pivotal role that the two years of XI and XII play in shaping a student's academic journey, especially for those in the Commerce stream. These formative years introduce students to subjects such as Accounts, Business Law, and Economics, which are crucial foundations for their future endeavors.

We prioritize building strong fundamentals as they are essential for success in the Std. XII Boards and lay the groundwork for exceptional performance in competitive exams like CA, CS, CMA, and BMM. Through our focused approach on concept clarity, disciplined study routines, and regular assessments, we ensure that our students develop the sharp acumen required to achieve high scores in these exams.

For Std. XI and XII, we offer comprehensive coaching programs catering to Maharashtra State Board, CBSE, and ISC syllabi. Our CBSE & ISC coaching is available nationwide through online lectures, providing flexibility and accessibility to students across the country. Students have the option to enroll in individual courses for Std. XI and Std. XII or opt for a combined course covering both years.

With decades of experience in coaching students for Boards, we are committed to ensuring our students' success by delivering the highest quality education. Our team comprises over 100 professionally qualified faculty members who possess extensive experience in guiding young minds through the rigors of Board examinations.

Below is a list of subjects offered under various Boards for each class. In addition to Commerce subjects, we also provide coaching for Language papers with options including Hindi, Marathi, French, and more.

We, at Vidhyashala Academy, are dedicated to nurturing the academic growth and future success of our students, providing them with the guidance and support they need to excel in their educational journey.
