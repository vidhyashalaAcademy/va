OUR KEY FEATURES....

Experienced Faculty

The faculty at Vidhyashala Academy comprises of IIT-ians and professors from other leading universities which will train students to tackle their hardest problems and there by helping them crack competitive exams.

Personalized Attention

Vidhyashala Academy have an outstanding and highly educated faculties who value individualized attention and facilitates student comprehension. Since small class size encourages learning, we have a batch size of only 30 students.

Unique Study Materials

Study material for Engineering,MHT-CET and JEE are abridged by Shelar sir whereas for other courses notes are compiled by our experienced faculty. Study Materials at Vidhyashala Academy is designed carefully after analyzing all the previous year question papers. This helps the students to understand the gist of the chapter better..

Regular Tests

Conducting a test is an important parameter to determine how well the student has understood a concept. All the papers are assessed by our experienced professors itself. All the tests so conducted are based on the current exam pattern.

Monthly Parents Meeting

Vidhyashala Academy conducts monthly parents meeting to discuss child’s progress and to find solutions about their academic problems.This conference supplement the information By focusing on students progress. Interactive Learning Environment

We, at Vidhyashala Academy make it a point to organise student-teacher interaction so as to make the learning process more engaging and fun. We also conduct paper discussion to make every student aware about their mistakes and provide them with the necessary solutions to avoid the same.
